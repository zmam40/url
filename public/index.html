Recognizing the rich cultural and spiritual diversity of students attending Purdue University, our religious student groups are committed to providing safe places to grow spiritually through worship, prayer, study, conversation,
and fellowship and to put faith into action through service opportunities, mission trips, and faith-based initiatives.

We invite you to check out the list of religious organizations for links to individual websites and contact information. You can click on locations to see locations of weekly meetings and/or offices for each group.
